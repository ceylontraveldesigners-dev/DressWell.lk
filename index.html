Fashion That Creates Opportunity

DressWell.lk is Sri Lanka's women-driven online fashion destination — built on the belief that great style and meaningful livelihoods go hand in hand.

Our Story

DressWell.lk was born from two convictions that most businesses keep separate: fashion should be accessible to everyone who wants to wear it, and opportunity should be available to every woman who has the talent to create it. In Sri Lanka, we saw skilled women — living at the poverty line, in villages and small towns far from economic centres — who had the hands, the patience, and the craft, but no platform to turn it into a livelihood.


So we built DressWell differently. Every garment sold on this platform is produced through our Artisan Collective — a program that recruits women from underprivileged communities, provides professional skills training in cutting, stitching, and finishing, and pays them fairly for work they can be proud of. For many of our artisans, DressWell is the first formal income they have ever earned. It is the first time their skill has been recognised and rewarded. And for some, it is the first step toward genuine independence — for themselves and for their families.

Our Values

At DressWell.lk, we believe a business has a responsibility that goes beyond its balance sheet. We are women-led from the very first stitch, built on the principle that style and social purpose are not opposites — they amplify each other. We offer fashion for everyone, across all genders and all ages, at honest LKR pricing that reflects real Sri Lankan lives rather than imported aspirations.


We commit to fast, island-wide delivery, hassle-free returns, and new arrivals every Friday — because our customers deserve reliability and excitement in equal measure. And behind every piece we deliver, we commit equally to the women who made it: fair wages, continuous skills development, and a working environment where they are respected, supported, and given room to grow. When a woman at DressWell grows, her family grows with her. That ripple effect is the business we are most proud of building.

The People Behind the Brand

Meet Our Team

Every DressWell piece begins with a person. Our team of six extraordinary women — from boardroom to workshop — is proof that when you invest in women, the results are beautiful.

Founder & Leadership
Founder & Chief Executive
Milani Wickramaratne

Milani launched DressWell.lk with a singular vision — to build Sri Lanka's first truly inclusive online fashion destination, and to prove that a business can be both profitable and purposeful. A driven entrepreneur with a background in business and marketing, she oversees the brand's strategy, growth, and community initiatives. The Artisan Collective — a skills development program that creates life-changing opportunities for talented women from underserved communities — is her most personal achievement. Milani leads DressWell not just as a business, but as a platform for women to break through the glass ceilings that have held them back for too long.

Design Team
Creative Director & Lead Designer
Ms. Kumari Perera

Kumari brings over a decade of fashion expertise to every DressWell collection. With a keen eye for global trends and a deep understanding of Sri Lankan aesthetics, she curates pieces that feel contemporary yet grounded in local identity. From silhouette selection to fabric sourcing, Kumari ensures every garment meets an exacting standard of style and wearability. Under her direction, DressWell's collections have found a voice that is uniquely Sri Lankan and undeniably modern.

Head of Atelier & Bespoke Design
Ms. Narmada Wickramasinghe

Narmada is the creative force behind DressWell's bespoke atelier. With formal training in couture construction and years of experience in made-to-order fashion, she leads every atelier piece from concept sketch to final fitting. Her signature blend of structured tailoring and graceful detailing has established DressWell Atelier as the destination of choice for bridal and special occasion wear in Sri Lanka. Narmada transforms each client's vision into a wearable piece of art.

The Artisan Collective

Our Artisan Collective is central to who we are. Three extraordinary women — from communities where skilled trades offered little to no opportunities for women — whose talent, resilience, and dedication bring every DressWell piece to life. Through our program, we provide professional training, fair wages, and a stage that celebrates their craft on a national level.

Artisan Collective
Pattern Cutting Specialist
Hirushi Perera

Hirushi's precision with pattern cutting is the silent foundation of every DressWell garment. Joining the Artisan Collective with limited formal training and a determination to build something for herself, she has since mastered garment construction with remarkable speed and accuracy. Her spatial intuition and relentless attention to detail make her an irreplaceable part of the DressWell team — and a role model for younger women in her community.

Artisan Collective
Master Seamstress
Ms. Dinuka Gamage

Dinuka's hands have stitched hundreds of DressWell pieces to perfection. A mother of two who came to the Artisan Collective seeking financial independence, she discovered not just a livelihood — but a calling. Her stitch work is meticulous, her consistency unmatched, and her quiet pride in every finished garment is a testament to what opportunity, combined with raw talent, can unlock in a person.

Artisan Collective
Senior Seamstress & Mentor
Mekala Gunawardena

Mekala represents everything DressWell's Artisan Collective stands for. Coming from a background with few prospects for women in skilled trades, she joined the program with basic sewing knowledge and an unshakeable drive to grow. Today, she handles complex assembly for the atelier collection and actively mentors newer team members. A natural leader who found her platform — and is using it to lift others.
